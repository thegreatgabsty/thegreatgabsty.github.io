about

gabrielle widjaja is a chinese-indonesian born, california-raised multidisciplinary graphic designer. her favorite typeface is cooper black.

gabby is currently a senior at the rhode island school of design and interned at stripe this summer! last year she worked at bbc worldwide. gabby designs for hack@brown, risd e'ship and is the graphic design director of brown political review. you can probably find her at CVS buying snacks.
