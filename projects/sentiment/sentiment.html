work

sentiment

This project was created for Color at RISD under the instruction of Aki Nurosi in the spring of 2017.

To wrap up the unit about positive and negative connotations of color being placed on top of white vs black, I created a box of chocolate bars. Each chocolate gives the consumer different emotional boosts (happiness, luck, cheerfulness, serenity) but has a black warning label on the back which lists side effects. For example, the red chocolate gives you more energy, but might also make you more irritable! Eat at your own risk.
